Iphone 14 pro max

Basic Overview

iPhone 14 pro max 5G has a display size of 6.7-inch. The touchscreen of it has ProMotion technology. The display also has a built-in crash detection technology. The front cam has 48 MP Truedepth came with an iconic photonic engine. This new model comes with one more hour of video playback in contrast to the former model. The camera also equipped with image stabilization, which helps in getting clearcut and unblurred images. And finally, coming to the brain of this iconic iphone 14 pro max, apple is offering A16 Bionic ship with 6gb RAM.
